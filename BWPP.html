with other Columbia College Chicago students. The Artist for all 2D Assets was Madeline Rogers,

Mechanics worked on and Implemented in game

  • Implemented Currency and XP system for player.
  • Implemented Complete Inventory and Menu system, Player can.
    open/navigate through menu,settings, and inventory as well as
    drop or use item selected.
  • Shop system, where player can sell and buy items from currency collected
  • Worked on already implemented interaction system, control player state and controls
    when interacting.
  • Implemented Player ability to climb and hide from monsters in scene
